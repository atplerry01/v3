# WHYCESPACE WBSM v3
# PHASE 2.0.38 — POLICY EVIDENCE RECORDER

You are implementing Phase 2.0.38 of the Whycespace system.

This phase introduces the **Policy Evidence Recorder**.

The evidence recorder captures immutable records of policy decisions and enforcement events.

These records provide governance evidence and will later be anchored to WhyceChain.
