# COMPONENT 2.2.28 — CAPITAL RECONCILIATION ENGINE

You are implementing the **Capital Reconciliation Engine**.

The Capital Reconciliation Engine verifies **consistency across capital records, balances, ledger entries, and financial events** within the Whycespace economic infrastructure.

The engine detects discrepancies between:

- capital registry records
- capital ledger entries
- pool balance calculations
- reservation records
- allocation records
- distribution records

The engine produces **reconciliation reports** highlighting inconsistencies.

The Capital Reconciliation Engine **does not mutate state and does not emit events**.

It performs deterministic analysis and returns reconciliation results.

This implementation must follow **Whycespace WBSM v3 strict architecture rules**.

If any rule cannot be satisfied you must STOP and request clarification.

Never guess architecture decisions.

## STEP 3 — PURPOSE

The Capital Reconciliation Engine verifies that all capital records remain consistent across the capital lifecycle.

Reconciliation ensures that:
- contributions match ledger entries
- reservations do not exceed available capital
- allocations align with reservation records
- utilization matches allocation amounts
- distributions match investor ownership records
