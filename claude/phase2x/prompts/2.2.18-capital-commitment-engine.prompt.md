# STEP 3 — PURPOSE OF CAPITAL COMMITMENT ENGINE

A **capital commitment** represents a formal pledge to invest capital into a pool.

Example:

Investor pledges £100,000 into Property Investment Pool A.

Commitment characteristics:

• capital not yet transferred
• tracked for allocation planning
• subject to validation and policy checks
• may later become a contribution

The engine performs:

• register commitment
• update commitment amount
• cancel commitment
• mark commitment fulfilled

Every mutation must emit a deterministic domain event.
