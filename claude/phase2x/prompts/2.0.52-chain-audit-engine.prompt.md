SECTION 1 — ARCHITECTURE VERIFICATION
------------------------------------------------------------

You are implementing the **Chain Audit Engine** for the WhyceChain system.

The Chain Audit Engine performs analytical evaluation of the chain to produce audit reports.

The engine helps detect:

- broken block linkage
- invalid block hashes
- Merkle root inconsistencies
- ledger sequence gaps
- unexpected entry ordering
- evidence anomalies
