SECTION 2 — COMPONENT PURPOSE
------------------------------------------------------------

The Policy Conflict Analysis Engine performs advanced analysis of policy conflicts.

This engine operates primarily during:

- policy simulation
- governance review
- policy deployment validation

Unlike the Policy Conflict Detection Engine (2.0.24), which detects direct conflicts,
this engine provides deeper conflict intelligence:

- cross-policy conflict patterns
- priority override chains
- governance escalation risks
- multi-domain conflict analysis

The engine produces governance intelligence reports.

3 detect dependency escalation patterns
4 cluster related conflicts
5 assign severity levels
6 generate governance recommendations

------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 8 — ENGINE RULES
------------------------------------------------------------

The engine must be:

- stateless
- deterministic
- thread-safe

The engine must NOT:

- persist data
- access infrastructure
- mutate policy definitions
- execute policy decisions

The engine performs analysis only.

------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 9 — EVENT BEHAVIOR
------------------------------------------------------------

This engine does not emit domain events.

Results are used by:

Policy Governance workflows
