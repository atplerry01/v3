SECTION 2 — COMPONENT PURPOSE
------------------------------------------------------------

The Policy Domain Binding Engine associates policies with specific governance domains across the Whycespace ecosystem.

Domains define where a policy is applied.

Examples of domains:

- cluster domains
- subcluster domains
- SPV governance domains
- vault operations
- identity governance
- workflow governance

The engine ensures that policies are evaluated only within their bound domains.

SECTION 7 — ENGINE IMPLEMENTATION
------------------------------------------------------------

Create file:

PolicyDomainBindingEngine.cs

Primary method:

BindPolicyToDomain(PolicyDomainBindingCommand command)

Algorithm:

1 validate policy exists
2 validate domain identifier
3 construct binding record
4 produce binding result

The engine validates the binding logic.

Binding persistence is handled by registry storage layers.

------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 8 — ENGINE RULES
------------------------------------------------------------

The engine must be:

- stateless
- deterministic
- thread-safe

The engine must NOT:

- persist domain bindings
- access infrastructure
- mutate policy definitions
- bypass lifecycle rules

The engine validates and produces binding results only.

------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 9 — EVENT BEHAVIOR
------------------------------------------------------------

This engine does not emit domain events.

Domain bindings are executed through the registry layer.
