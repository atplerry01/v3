------------------------------------------------------------
SYSTEM CONTEXT
------------------------------------------------------------

The **Identity Policy Enforcement Adapter** integrates the WhyceID
identity system with **WHYCEPOLICY™**, the constitutional policy engine.

The adapter ensures that identity-related operations are validated
against active policy rules before execution.

Typical policy enforcement scenarios:

• identity creation restrictions
• role assignment validation
• permission enforcement
• device registration policy checks
• identity revocation authorization
• identity recovery eligibility
• trust score thresholds

The adapter does not evaluate policies itself.

It forwards evaluation requests to the **Policy Evaluation Engine**
within the WHYCEPOLICY system.
