# 2.0.22 — Policy Registry

## Component
- **Name:** Policy Registry
- **System Category:** WhycePolicy System
- **Architecture Layer:** System Layer
- **Engine Tier:** N/A
- **Implementation Category:** SYSTEM MODEL

## Purpose
The Policy Registry maintains the authoritative catalog of all policy definitions used by the WhycePolicy system. It supports registering policies, retrieving policies by domain/ID, retrieving active policies, tracking policy versions, and tracking policy lifecycle state.
