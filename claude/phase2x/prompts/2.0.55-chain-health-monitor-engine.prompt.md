## Description
The Chain Health Monitor Engine evaluates operational health of the WhyceChain
by inspecting block continuity, ledger entry integrity, snapshot consistency,
replication synchronization, and evidence anchoring status. The engine generates
health reports classified as Healthy, Degraded, or Critical but does NOT modify
chain data.

## Health Evaluation Rules
- Block Continuity: sequential block heights + previous block hash linkage
- Ledger Integrity: ledger entry sequence continuity
- Replication Status: ReplicationLag = CurrentChainHeight - ReplicationHeight
- Anchoring Status: AnchorLag = CurrentChainHeight - AnchoredBlockHeight
- Snapshot Status: snapshot height must not exceed chain height

## Health Classification
- Healthy: no integrity issues, minimal replication/anchor lag
- Degraded: moderate replication lag, delayed anchoring, snapshot lag
- Critical: broken block linkage, ledger sequence gaps, invalid hashes

## Engine Contract
Stateless, deterministic, thread-safe. No infrastructure dependencies.
