SECTION 2 — COMPONENT PURPOSE
------------------------------------------------------------

The Policy Evidence Recorder produces cryptographic evidence records for all policy governance operations.

Evidence records allow Whycespace to verify governance actions with immutable integrity.

Evidence records are generated for:

• policy lifecycle transitions
• policy evaluation decisions
• policy enforcement outcomes
• governance approvals
• policy activation events

Evidence data is later anchored to **WhyceChain** for immutable audit verification.

SECTION 8 — ENGINE RULES
------------------------------------------------------------

The engine must be:

• stateless
• deterministic
• thread-safe

The engine must NOT:

• persist evidence records
• access infrastructure
• mutate policy definitions
• execute governance decisions

Evidence persistence and anchoring are handled by:

WhyceChain integration layer.

------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 9 — EVENT BEHAVIOR
------------------------------------------------------------
