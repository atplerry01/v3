SECTION 2 — COMPONENT PURPOSE
------------------------------------------------------------

The Policy Dependency Engine resolves dependency relationships between policies.

Policies may depend on other policies being evaluated first.

Examples:

• guardian approval policy depends on identity verification policy
• capital movement policy depends on vault compliance policy
• cluster governance policy depends on cluster authorization policy

The engine determines a **deterministic evaluation order** for policies.

The engine must detect:

• missing dependencies
• circular dependencies
• dependency violations

The engine produces a **resolved policy execution order**.

------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 7 — ENGINE RULES
------------------------------------------------------------

This engine must follow WBSM v3 execution rules.

The engine must be:

• stateless
• deterministic
• thread-safe

The engine must NOT:

• persist data
• call other engines
• access infrastructure
• mutate domain aggregates

The engine performs dependency resolution only.
