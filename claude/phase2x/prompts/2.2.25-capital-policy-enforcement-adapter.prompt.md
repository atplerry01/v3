# WHYCESPACE WBSM v3
# CAPITAL SYSTEM
# COMPONENT 2.2.25 — CAPITAL POLICY ENFORCEMENT ADAPTER

You are implementing the **Capital Policy Enforcement Adapter**.

The Capital Policy Enforcement Adapter integrates the Capital System with **WHYCEPOLICY™**, ensuring that all capital-related operations comply with the constitutional governance rules of Whycespace.

Capital operations subject to policy enforcement include:

- capital commitments
- capital contributions
- capital reservations
- capital allocations
- capital utilizations
- capital distributions

This adapter acts as the **policy gateway** for capital workflows.

The adapter must follow **Whycespace WBSM v3 strict architecture rules**.

## Files

- `CapitalPolicyContext.cs` — Policy context model with operation type, pool, investor, amount, currency, cluster, SPV fields
- `CapitalPolicyDecision.cs` — Immutable decision result (IsAllowed, DecisionReason, PolicyId, EvaluatedAt)
- `CapitalPolicyRequest.cs` — Request bridge to WHYCEPOLICY (PolicyDomain, Operation, CapitalContext, Metadata)
- `CapitalPolicyEnforcementAdapter.cs` — Stateless adapter that builds context, calls WHYCEPOLICY, returns decisions

## Policy Evaluation Flow

1. Capital operation request
2. Policy context generated
3. CapitalPolicyEnforcementAdapter
4. WHYCEPOLICY evaluation
5. Policy decision returned
6. Workflow execution allowed or denied

## Constraints

- Adapter is stateless, deterministic, thread-safe
- No business logic inside adapter
- No persistence inside adapter
- No domain state mutation
- WHYCEPOLICY is the authoritative policy engine
