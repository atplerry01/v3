# STEP 3 — PURPOSE OF CAPITAL RESERVATION ENGINE

A **capital reservation** temporarily locks a portion of available pool capital for a specific purpose.

Examples:

• reserving capital for SPV property acquisition
• reserving capital for asset purchase
• reserving capital for operational deployment
• reserving capital for contract obligations

Reservation characteristics:

• reduces available pool balance
• not yet allocated or deployed
• reversible until allocation occurs
• prevents capital overcommitment

Reservations are a **critical control to ensure capital integrity**.

Every reservation must emit deterministic events.
