
## Reflective Account – Supporting Personal Care for Mrs B

During my support visit with **Mrs B**, an elderly lady with reduced mobility and body pain, my aim was to assist her with her personal care needs while promoting **dignity, independence, comfort, and safety**.

### Supporting Mrs B to Use Toilet Facilities

When I arrived at Mrs B’s home, I first signed in according to workplace procedures. I then went upstairs to greet Mrs B and her daughter, who lives with her. Maintaining respectful communication is important to build trust and ensure the individual feels comfortable.

Before assisting Mrs B, I washed and dried my hands thoroughly and put on the appropriate **Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)** including gloves and an apron, in line with infection control policies.

Mrs B experiences pain in her shoulders and knees, and her mobility is weak. I carefully assisted her out of bed, ensuring I supported her safely while encouraging her to remain as independent as possible. I helped her put on her shoes and encouraged her to walk slowly using her **Zimmer frame** while I supported her to the bathroom.

Once in the bathroom, I ensured she was comfortable and safe by assisting her to sit on the **bathroom commode**. I stayed nearby to ensure I could respond quickly if she needed help while maintaining her **privacy and dignity**.

After she finished using the toilet, I supported her to clean herself using the most appropriate and hygienic method. I ensured any bodily waste was managed discreetly and safely to minimise the risk of cross infection and to respect Mrs B’s dignity and personal preferences.

---

### Supporting Mrs B to Maintain Personal Hygiene

I supported Mrs B with a **full body shower**. Throughout the process I encouraged her to participate as much as possible to maintain her independence.

I washed her hair using shampoo and conditioner and helped wash her body using appropriate equipment including a sponge and towel. Particular attention was given to personal areas to ensure cleanliness and comfort. After washing, I carefully rinsed and dried her hair and body.

I ensured the bathroom was safe and that water temperature was suitable for her comfort and safety. I also ensured all toiletries and materials were placed within reach and used safely.

After her shower, I assisted Mrs B back to her bedroom using the wheelchair where necessary. I supported her to get dressed while encouraging her to choose the clothes she wanted to wear. Promoting choice helps maintain the individual’s dignity and independence.

Once dressed, I assisted her with grooming and personal hygiene including applying cream, talcum powder, roll-on deodorant, and combing her hair.

---

### Supporting Mrs B to Manage Her Personal Appearance

While assisting Mrs B with dressing and grooming, I maintained friendly communication with her to ensure she felt comfortable and respected. I encouraged her to make decisions about her clothing and appearance.

I ensured all materials and equipment used were left clean, tidy, and ready for future use. I also maintained good personal hygiene by washing my hands before and after providing care.

---

### Supporting with Toileting and Oral Care

After Mrs B was dressed, I supported her to use the bathroom again for her convenience. While she was there, she brushed her teeth. Mrs B prefers to complete her personal care before brushing her teeth, and I respected her preferences.

During this time, I briefly tidied her bedroom and made her bed. After she finished in the bathroom, I assisted her with cleaning herself and helped her put on her underwear and trousers again.

---

### Supporting Mobility and Comfort

After her personal care routine, I supported Mrs B to the **stair lift**, ensuring she was seated comfortably and safely before it took her downstairs.

Once downstairs, I supported her using her Zimmer frame to walk safely to the living room where she sat comfortably with her daughter to watch television.

---

### Meal Preparation

Before assisting with breakfast, I washed and dried my hands again and changed my gloves to maintain hygiene standards.

I helped prepare a light breakfast for Mrs B, which included **cereal, fruits, and a cup of tea**. I placed a **bib** on her to protect her clean clothes from stains while she ate.

---

### Supporting Medication

I assisted Mrs B by helping her pop out her medication from the packaging and encouraged her to take it in my presence, ensuring she followed her prescribed medication routine safely.

---

### Recording and Ending the Visit

After completing all tasks, I spent a few minutes chatting with Mrs B to provide emotional support and companionship. I then recorded all the care I had provided in the care notes according to workplace procedures.

Finally, I said goodbye to Mrs B and her daughter and signed out before leaving.
