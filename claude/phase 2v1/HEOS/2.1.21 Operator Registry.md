# WHYCESPACE WBSM v3
# HEOS — HUMAN ECONOMIC OPERATING SYSTEM
# 2.1.21 OPERATOR REGISTRY

You are implementing the **Operator Registry** inside the Whycespace system.

The Operator Registry is part of **HEOS (Human Economic Operating System)**.

Operators represent participants who possess **operational authority** to supervise workforce, manage assignments, and control operational activities within clusters and SPVs.

Operators are responsible for:

• workforce supervision  
• assignment approval  
• operational coordination  
• compliance oversight  

Participants must first exist in the **Participant Registry** before becoming operators.

This implementation must follow **WBSM v3 Prompt Structural Rules and Guardrails**.

# STEP 4 — DOMAIN VALUE OBJECTS

Define the following primitives.

---

## OperatorId

Strongly typed identifier.

Fields:

Id (GUID)

Must be immutable.

---

## OperatorLevel

Represents level of operational authority.

Examples:

Supervisor  
Manager  
Director  
SystemOperator  

Higher levels possess broader control privileges.

---

## OperatorStatus

Represents lifecycle state of an operator.

Statuses:

Pending  
Active  
Suspended  
Revoked  

Suspended operators cannot perform operational actions.

---

## OperatorScope

Defines operational coverage.

Examples:

ClusterScope  
SubClusterScope  
SPVScope  
GlobalScope  

Scope determines where the operator has authority.

---

OperatorAggregate.cs

---

# STEP 6 — AGGREGATE OPERATIONS

Implement domain operations.

RegisterOperator

Creates operator record for a participant.

AssignScope

Adds operational scope.

RemoveScope

Removes scope.

ActivateOperator

Transitions status to Active.

SuspendOperator

Suspends operational privileges.

RevokeOperator

Permanently removes operator authority.

All operations must enforce domain invariants.

---

# STEP 7 — DOMAIN INVARIANTS

Enforce the following rules:

Participant must exist before operator registration.

Operator must have at least one scope.

Suspended operators cannot receive new scopes.

Revoked operators cannot be reactivated.

Operator level determines allowed scopes.

# STEP 9 — ENGINE INTERACTION RULE

Domain must never invoke engines.

Incorrect:

OperatorAggregate → Engine

Engines operate on the aggregate externally.
